ISLAMIC SCHOLAR PROGRAM
ABOUT
This program consists of part-time classes catered towards individuals who wish to study various Islamic sciences and become scholars of Islam (Alim) while working full time or taking care of a family. The curriculum includes two years of training in the Arabic language, followed by an in-depth study of well known books of Islamic Jurisprudence, Creed, Teachings of Prophet Muhammad ﷺ, and other subjects. Classes take place on weeknights and Sundays.
