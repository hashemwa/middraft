QURAN ACADEMY
ABOUT
Embark on a journey with Mufti Masihullah Bhaiyat, a distinguished reciter of the Quran who has mastered seven different Qirats. This program emphasizes on the art of memorization and the precise rules of Quranic recitation, known as Tajweed. Whether you are seeking a full-time or part-time commitment, our program offers both options. Experience the deep fulfillment of connecting with the Quran and understanding that Allah holds a special love for those who commit to memorizing His sacred text.
خَيْرُكُم مَّنْ تَعَلَّمَ الْقُرْآنَ وَ عَلَّمَهُ
“The best of you is the one who learns the Quran and teaches it”
Prophet Muhammad ﷺ
