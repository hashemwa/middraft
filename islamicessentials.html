ISLAMIC ESSENTIALS
ABOUT
The Sanatain program is an intensive course on the essentials of Islamic Studies that has been exclusively designed for women, men, new Muslims, university students, professionals, and teens who want to learn more about Islam. The aim of the course, which runs over a period of two years, is to provide a holistic approach to Islam and aid in embedding the concepts of Islam into our daily lives.
